How do I get started?

This one should be too easy to explain but here are some hints if you would need them.

  1. Start Lustro, which you have or you wouldn't be reading this.
  2. Choose the export formats you want. You need to select at least on but can select all if you want.
  3. Click the lock icon and enter your Google username and password and click ‘Sign In’ if you selected the Google option. You won't be able to export your contacts to Google if you are not signed in. You may choose to save your Google credentials in Keychain so that you don't have to enter it again.
  4. Press the ‘Export’ and wait till the exports are finished.
  5. A small icon besides each selected export shows the status of the export. Once done it indicates if this export was successful or not.
  6. Check possible warnings or errors (if you see a warning or error icon) in the log file (Window → Show Log).
